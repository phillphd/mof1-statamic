<p>Dave is a veteran illustrator that got his start on Tiny Toons. Rad. If I had to pick two words to describe Dave's work they would be expressive and variety. He continues to grow his skill by creating challenges for himself on new project, and his attitude behind maintaining motivation is one to be emulated. There is a lot to learn from Dave and we only scratched the surface. Ready for the next one.</p>

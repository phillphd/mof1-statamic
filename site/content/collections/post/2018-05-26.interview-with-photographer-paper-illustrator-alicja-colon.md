<p>Be bold enough to go after your purpose. Back in April, I had the opportunity to talk at length with Alicja about the relaunch of Manual Focus and its new direction. While she initially had a clear calling for the show, it took several years for her to embrace that higher purpose and confidently pursue it. Today we discuss the new iteration of Manual Focus and take a look at the incredible photography and paper illustration that is dominating her social feeds. It's a surprisingly calm and focused discussion given all the personality sitting around the table.</p>

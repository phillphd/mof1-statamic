<p>Thanks to a connection through /r/posternews, this week we are sitting down with the supremely talented commercial illustrator Robert Bruno. Certainly Bruno has an impressive client list (Disney, Marvel, Lucasfilm, ESPN, to name a few), but I walked away from this interview more impressed with how easy Bruno makes a conversation. We drift from his style to sports, detour to The Office, bring it back to scheduling and finish up with talk of lighting progression. Levity abounds and so does casual wisdom. Definitely want to listen to this one.</p>

<p>Put your pens and styli down, take a step back from your task list and question your perspective. Jamal has decided to devote his life to mentoring a younger generation and giving them the skills they need to rise above their circumstances. These are kids that otherwise would be absorbed by the party culture and in many cases without modern skills to build a future on. It is absolutely important we each question how we can leverage our knowledge, experience, and strong footing to pull up those around us. Use what you know to give someone else a chance they might not have otherwise.</p>
